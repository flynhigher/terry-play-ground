The following guidelines are designed to help provide a quality environment for our users. Please take a minute to read them and keep them in mind when you are setting up your profile.

We welcome all questions and commentary, including constructive feedback. We do expect that users upload content and images that are both relevant and respectful to this community as a whole. We reserve the right to remove any content or image that does not adhere to our guidelines and to block anyone who violates them repeatedly.

Specifically, we do not tolerate these kinds of uploads:

  • No nudity or other sexually explicit content
  • No content that contains hate speech
  • No content that attacks an individual or group
  • No content that contains self harm or excessive violence
  • No content that contains illegal drug use
 To upload photos, take the following steps:
  • Go to your profile.
  • Enter personal information that include, your email, password, address and phone number
  • Upload image: ensure images are the correct format. We accepts jpg, jpeg, gif, png file formats. Image size must not exceed 1MB
  • Enter questions / comments: You are limited to 500 characters
  • Check whether you wish to make profile public or not
  • Save profile

Termination

If you violate the letter or spirit of this Statement, or otherwise create risk or possible legal exposure for us, we reserve the right to revoke your membership from the website. We will notify you of your termination by email.
