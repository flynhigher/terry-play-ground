ServSafe Certification, brought to you by Alpha Management.

The ServSafe food manager certification from Alpha Management, will give your restaurant a recognized mark of excellence in food service safety. Developed in partnership with the NRA (National Restaurant Association, USA) and the USDA (United States Department of Agriculture), along with other leading U.S. regulatory authorities, the ServSafe curriculum brought to you by Alpha Management is a comprehensive training program on the most up-to-date knowledge and techniques for food safety.

Graduates of the training will become able administrators of food establishment and knowledgeable in proper hygiene, sanitation, purchasing, receiving, storage, cooking, preparation, service, pest control, hazard control, and regulatory awareness. The Alpha program delivers high quality training, examination, and certification through top-notch instructors, audio/video aids, class discussion, role playing, and more. Classes are fun and engaging. Exams are available same day as training is completed, and certifications delivered promptly.

Who should receive the training?

The program is relevant to everyone in the food service organization, since all members are responsible for safe food handling. Owners, purchasing and receiving staff, service managers, prep cooks, chefs, food servers, and dishwashers... anyone directly or indirectly involved with the food operation can be certified and can benefit from the training. It's up to you who will receive the training.
