About Us

Alpha Management Solution serves Virginia, Maryland, Delaware, New York, and Pennsylvania with ServSafe Training, HACCP Analysis, and more for your restaurant or food service buisness. We servce up success ... GUARANTEED! Whether you're an established operation or a new restaurant, you need to protect your business and your customers from the threat of foodbourne illness. Alpha Management Solution is the answer! We provide the following services:

Servsafe Education and Training

 Servsafe begins with a first-rate trainings program that will equip your employees to incorporate safe practices in every aspect of your food service operation. We will train your employees to recognize and prevent the hazards tht cause food to become unsafe. We have the latest curriculum from the National Restaurant Association's program with proven teaching methods that take the mystery out of the science of safe food handling while giving an in-depth understanding.

HACCP Programs

 Alpha Management Solution also offers operational services including Audit Preparation, Risk Monitoring, and HACCP Programs. We will analyze your systems, identify Critical Control Points (CCP), and work with you to develop tight procedures for hazard monitoring, control, and correction. With HACCP you won't have to wonder whether you are controlling yoursafety hazards, you'll know for sure.
