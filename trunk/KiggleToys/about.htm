Kiggle designs and produces infant and preschool toys inspired by
creative new mom who studied early childhood education and toy
design with real world experience in making toys for several years.

Now it is time for her to create exceptional product designs for her
own kids and she would like to share her toys with all other children.

There are millions of toys out there, but not many toys are satisfied
by busy parents and new kids' exploration and curiosity to real world.
Kiggle's mission is to make a safe, practical, long-lasting, and
educational toys by innovative and contemporary design based on
parenthood experience.

Kids' giggle!
Kiggle toys will give our children a promising and bright smile.
